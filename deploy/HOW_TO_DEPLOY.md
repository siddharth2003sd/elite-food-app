# How to Upload Your Files (Permanent 24/7 Link)

Since Firebase Hosting requires a special terminal tool to upload, the easiest way to get your site online **forever** for free is using **GitHub Pages** or **Netlify**.

### Option 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended & Permanent)
1.  Go to [GitHub.com](https://github.com/) and log in (or create a free account).
2.  Click the **"+"** icon in the top right and select **"New repository"**.
3.  Give it a name (like `my-project`), set it to **Public**, and click **"Create repository"**.
4.  On the next screen, look for the link that says **"uploading an existing file"**.
5.  **Drag and drop** everything inside your `d:\MiniProject\deploy` folder into the browser.
6.  Once they upload, scroll down and click **"Commit changes"**.
7.  Go to **Settings** (top tab) -> **Pages** (left sidebar).
8.  Under "Branch", select **main** and click **Save**.
9.  Wait 1-2 minutes, and your site will be live at `https://yourname.github.io/my-project/`.

### Option 2: Netlify Drop (Fastest - Just Drag & Drop)
1.  Go to [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop).
2.  **Drag and drop** your entire `deploy` folder into the big box on the screen.
3.  Your site is live **instantly**! 
4.  *Note: To keep the link from expiring, you should click "Sign Up" after it uploads.*
